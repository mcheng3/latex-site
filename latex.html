What is Latex?

  • LaTeX is a markup language.
  • Formats plain text into a styled document.
  • Allows you to focus on the content of your document by taking care of the formatting for you. This is unlike a WYSIWYG editor, where the user has to directly format the entire document.
  • Useful for documents which require heavy use of special characters.
