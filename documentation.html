Text Formatting

Here are some examples of formatting choices:
  • Font-size:
    • 	  \begin{tiny} hello \end{tiny}
	  \begin{small} hello \end{small}
	  \begin{large} hello \end{large}
	  \begin{huge} hello \end{huge}
  • Italicize:
    • 	  \textit{word}
  • Bold:
    • 	  \textbf{word}
  • Center text:
    • 	  \begin{center} Title \end{center}

Packages

  • Packages in latex are similar to java and python packages.
  • At the beginning of your code, insert:
    • 	  \usepackage{}
  • Here are some examples of how to use packages:
    • 	  \usepackage[top=1in, bottom=2in]{geometry} #sets up margins

	  \usepackage{siunitx} #special math characters
	  \SI{5}{\micro\meter}

	  \usepackage{amsfonts} #additional special characters
	  $\mathbb{N}$
	  $\mathbb{R}$
 
	  \usepackage{graphicx} #inserts an image
	  \includegraphics{image.png}
